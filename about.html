About John Mwaura

A leader shaped by the struggles and hopes of our people

Who Is John Kiongozi?

Hon. John Mwaura (JM) is a businessman, investor, and emerging leader born in Thika, residing in Juja, and a registered voter in Ruiru, Kiambu County.

He has built a strong reputation through investments spanning Kiambu County, the Mt. Kenya region, across Kenya, and in international markets.

Committed to inclusive economic growth and transformative leadership, Hon. John Mwaura is passionate about creating opportunities, promoting enterprise, and advancing sustainable development.

His leadership is anchored on integrity, innovation, and a vision of a prosperous Kiambu that serves as a model for regional and national progress.

His Journey

2005

Community Leader

Founded a youth empowerment organization that trained over 2,000 young people in vocational skills.

2010

Ward Representative

Elected as MCA and oversaw construction of 3 dispensaries and 12km of rural roads.

2017

County Assembly Speaker

Championed the county's first youth fund, disbursing KSh 50M to over 500 youth groups.

2027

Governor — The Next Step

Ready to take the county to the next level with a bold, people-first agenda.
