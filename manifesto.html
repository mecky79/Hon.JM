The Manifesto

A clear, bold agenda for every ward in our county

This manifesto is a contract with the people. Every promise here is backed by a concrete plan, a budget allocation, and a timeline. We are not here to campaign — we are here to govern.

Pillar 1 — Youth Empowerment & Jobs

50,000 Jobs in 5 Years

Through partnerships with private sector companies, county tenders reserved for youth-owned businesses, and a skills training fund targeting every sub-county.

Youth Enterprise Fund

KSh 500M fund disbursed annually to registered youth groups — accessible through a transparent online application system.

TVET Sponsorships

1,000 sponsored placements per year at county-supported Technical and Vocational Training institutes.

Pillar 2 — Healthcare For All

Equip Every Sub-County Hospital

Full equipment upgrade for all sub-county hospitals including ICU units, theatre facilities, and reliable ambulance services.

Free Maternal Healthcare

Zero cost delivery services for all women across every public health facility in the county.

Community Health Promoters

Deploy and pay 2,000 community health workers — one per village — to bring healthcare to the doorstep.

Pillar 3 — Agriculture & Food Security

Subsidized Farm Inputs

Seeds, fertilizer, and pesticides at 50% subsidy for every registered smallholder farmer in the county.

Irrigation Infrastructure

Develop irrigation schemes covering 10,000 acres of arable land in drought-prone wards.

County Market Upgrades

Modernize 20 key markets to reduce post-harvest losses and give farmers better access to buyers.

Pillar 4 — Infrastructure & Connectivity

Roads Tarmacking Programme

500km of key roads tarmacked over 5 years, prioritizing market access roads and school routes.

Clean Water For All Wards

Piped water connections reaching every ward headquarters, with boreholes for remote villages.

Public WiFi Hubs

Free public WiFi installed at every sub-county headquarters, market, and TVET institution to support the digital economy.
